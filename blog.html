Embrace Retro Vibes with Acid Wash Shirts from Xen!

Fashion is cyclical, and one trend that’s making a powerful comeback is the acid wash shirt. If you’re looking to add a touch of vintage flair to your wardrobe, Xen has you covered with our unique collection of acid wash shirts. Here’s why these retro-inspired pieces are a must-have for any fashion-forward individual.

The Allure of Acid Wash

Acid wash shirts originated in the 1980s and quickly became a symbol of edgy, rebellious style. The distinctive mottled pattern is created through a unique process involving pumice stones and chlorine. This results in each shirt having its own one-of-a-kind look, perfect for those who love to stand out from the crowd.

Why Choose Acid Wash Shirts?

  • Unique Aesthetic: No two acid wash shirts are exactly the same. Each piece in our collection boasts its own unique pattern, ensuring you’ll have a truly one-of-a-kind item.
  • Versatility: These shirts can be dressed up or down. Pair with jeans for a casual day out, or layer under a blazer for an effortlessly cool evening look.
  • Comfort: Made from high-quality, breathable materials, our acid wash shirts are as comfortable as they are stylish.

How to Style Acid Wash Shirts

  • Casual Look: For a laid-back vibe, pair your acid wash shirt with ripped jeans and sneakers. Add a pair of aviator sunglasses for a touch of rockstar cool.
  • Smart Casual: Tuck your shirt into chinos and finish with a belt and loafers. This balances the bold pattern of the shirt with a more refined bottom half, perfect for a casual office setting or a night out.
  • Layering: Use your acid wash shirt as a layering piece. Wear it open over a plain white tee or under a denim jacket for a stylish, multi-dimensional look.
